🔍 How Do We Estimate Cracking Time?

We use three main things to guess how long it would take for a computer to "brute-force" your password:

  • 🔢 Password Length – More characters = more combinations!
  • 🔠 Character Variety – Does your password have lowercase, UPPERCASE, numbers, and symbols?
  • ⚙️ Attacker's Power – Are they using a slow CPU or a powerful GPU?

We multiply all possible combinations and divide them by how fast the computer can guess. That gives us an estimate!
